01

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how LinkIT360 ("we," "us," or "our"), a company based in Indonesia, operates the mobile game Lunar Path (the "Game") available on the Google Play Store.

This Policy explains what information is (and is not) collected when you play Lunar Path, how any data is handled, and the rights you have as a user — regardless of where you are located in the world.

✅ Privacy-first design: Lunar Path does not collect, store, or process any personal information from its players. Our game is built on a privacy-by-design principle — you play, and your data stays yours.

By downloading and playing Lunar Path, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this Policy, please discontinue use of the Game.

02

Information We Collect

🔒 We do not collect any personal data. LinkIT360 does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information to play Lunar Path.

We do not collect, request, receive, store, or process the following from users:

  • Name, email address, or other contact information
  • Device identifiers (IDFA, GAID, IMEI, or similar)
  • Location data (GPS or approximate)
  • Usage or behavioral analytics
  • Gameplay statistics sent to our servers
  • Crash reports or diagnostics sent to us
  • Financial information or payment data
  • Social media profiles or linked accounts
  • Photos, microphone, or camera data
  • Contacts or address book data

All game progress, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

ℹ️ Note on Advertising: While we do not collect data ourselves, the third-party advertising SDK integrated in this Game may collect certain technical information to serve ads. Please refer to Section 3 (Advertising) for full details.

Children's Privacy

Lunar Path is designed to be accessible to users of all ages, including children under the age of 13. We take children's online privacy extremely seriously and comply with all applicable laws and platform policies in this regard.

👦 COPPA Compliance (USA): In compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The Game requires no registration, account creation, or submission of personal information.
🇪🇺 GDPR-K Compliance (EU): In compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation as applied to children (GDPR-K), and in accordance with national age thresholds across EU member states, Lunar Path does not process personal data of children. No parental consent mechanisms are required as we do not collect any data.

Our commitments regarding children include:

  • No collection of personal information from any user, including children

  • No social features, chat, or user-generated content

  • Advertising configured for child-directed, non-personalized delivery only
  • No behavioral tracking or profiling of any user

If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe your child has been exposed to inappropriate content or you have concerns about this Game, please contact us immediately at jawarajoystudio@gmail.com.

Google Play Families Policy Compliance

LinkIT360 is fully committed to complying with the Google Play Families Policy. As a game accessible to children under 13, we adhere to the following requirements:

🛡️ Lunar Path participates in the Google Play Families program and meets all associated content, advertising, and data handling requirements applicable to apps designed for children.

Our Play Families Policy commitments include:

  • Content appropriateness: All in-game content is appropriate for users of all ages. No violence, adult themes, or inappropriate language is present in the Game.
  • Child-directed advertising: Ads are served in child-directed mode with all personalization and behavioral targeting features disabled. Only ad networks and formats compliant with the Families Policy are used.
  • No data collection: We do not collect personal data from any user, fully satisfying the Families Policy data restrictions.
  • No deceptive design: The Game does not employ deceptive user interfaces, dark patterns, or manipulative design that could take advantage of younger users.
  • No social networking: The Game contains no chat features, user profiles, or social interactions that could expose children to contact with strangers.
  • No in-app purchases: The Game requires no payment and contains no virtual currency purchases, loot boxes, or similar monetization features.
  • Parental gates: Any links to external content (such as our developer contact or app store rating prompts) are placed behind appropriate parental acknowledgment mechanisms where required.

We commit to maintaining these standards through all future updates to Lunar Path and will update this Privacy Policy to reflect any material changes.

Because Lunar Path does not collect any personal data from users, no personal data is processed by LinkIT360, and there is no data to access, rectify, erase, or port. However, we recognize the following GDPR rights and commit to respecting them fully:

Your Right What It Means Applicable to Lunar Path

Data Security

Because Lunar Path does not collect, transmit, or store personal data on our servers, the risk of a data breach involving user personal information from our Game is effectively eliminated at the data collection layer.

Game data stored locally on your device (such as progress and scores) is subject to your device's own security mechanisms. We recommend keeping your device's operating system and security software up to date to protect data stored on your device.

In the unlikely event that we discover any security vulnerability that could affect user privacy, we commit to investigating and resolving it promptly, and to notifying affected parties and relevant authorities where required by applicable law.
