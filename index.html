Introduction

Welcome to Lunar Path – an exciting space adventure game! We're committed to protecting your privacy while you enjoy your cosmic journey.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game on Google Play Store. We believe in transparency and keeping things simple.

🔒 Our Commitment: We don't collect personal information like your name, email, or location. Your privacy is our priority.

What We Do NOT Collect

Lunar Path is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what we never collect:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your precise location data
  • Your device contacts or calendar information
  • Your files, photos, or media
  • Your account credentials or authentication data
  • Any gameplay data that identifies you personally
  • Biometric information

Information We May Automatically Receive

🎯 Advertising ID

To show you relevant ads, we use your device's Advertising ID – a unique, non-personal identifier that doesn't reveal who you are.

  • Display personalized advertisements
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent fraud and duplicate ads
  • Comply with advertising standards
💡 You're in Control: Want to opt out of personalized ads?
  1. Open Settings > Google > Manage Your Google Account
  2. Go to Data & Privacy
  3. Select Opt out of Ads Personalization

🔗 Third-Party Ad Networks

We partner with ad networks to display ads. They may collect:

  • Device model and OS version
  • Ad interaction data (views and clicks)
  • General region/country location (not precise)
  • Aggregated usage statistics

Learn more about how Google handles your data.

📊 Device Information

We collect basic technical data to keep Lunar Path running smoothly:

  • Your device model and brand
  • Android operating system version
  • Game version you're using
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Gameplay duration and session frequency (anonymized)

How We Use Information

✨ Better Advertising

We use your Advertising ID to show you ads you might enjoy, not random content. We also prevent ad fraud and improve our campaigns.

🎮 Improving the Game

Your technical data helps us:

  • Fix bugs and crashes
  • Optimize performance on different devices
  • Understand how players use the game (in aggregate)
  • Make Lunar Path better with each update

⚖️ Legal Compliance

We follow laws, enforce our terms, and protect everyone from fraud and abuse.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect the technical data we receive. While no internet transmission is 100% secure, we do our best to safeguard your information.

⚠️ Important: Since we don't collect personal data, there's very little sensitive information at risk.

Children's Privacy

Lunar Path is designed for everyone, including kids. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and Google Play's Families Policy.

  • ✓ We don't knowingly collect personal info from kids under 13
  • ✓ No account registration required to play
  • ✓ No personal data requests
  • ✓ Family-friendly ad policies

Parents & Guardians: You can enable Google Play parental controls and adjust device advertising settings anytime.

Your Privacy Rights

🎯 Control Your Ads

  • Opt out of personalized ads in device settings
  • Reset your Advertising ID
  • View and manage your Google Advertising preferences

🔐 App Permissions

You can review and control which permissions Lunar Path has access to in your device settings. We keep our permission requirements minimal.

📞 Questions About Privacy?

We're here to help! Contact us anytime with questions or concerns.

Legal Compliance

🇪🇺 GDPR (European Users)

If you're in the EU, here's what you need to know:

  • We don't collect GDPR-protected personal data
  • Your Advertising ID may be protected – you can manage it through Google
  • You can file complaints with your local data protection authority

🇺🇸 CCPA (California Users)

California has strong privacy laws. Here's our compliance:

  • We don't "sell" personal information
  • We don't "share" data for behavioral ads
  • Your Advertising ID is protected under CPRA
  • Contact us to exercise your rights
